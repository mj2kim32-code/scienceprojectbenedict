Hypothesis If different metals (Aluminum, Zinc, Iron, Nickel, Lead, Copper) are mixed with Sulfuric Acid, then the metals will react at different rates according to the metal reactivity series (Zn > Fe > Ni > Pb > Cu > Al), because more reactive metals lose electrons more easily and are oxidized faster in redox reactions, causing them to react more quickly with Sulfuric Acid than less reactive metals.
Research Question How does the type of metal (Nickel, Zinc, Iron, Lead, Copper, and Aluminum) affect the rate of reaction with sulfuric acid, measured by the volume of hydrogen gas produced over a fixed period of time?
